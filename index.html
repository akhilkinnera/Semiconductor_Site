Powering Arizona’s High-Tech Future

The NAU Semiconductor Metrology Lab is designed to be the launchpad for the next generation of process technicians and engineers. We go beyond textbook theory, providing students access to industrial-grade optical, laser, pressure, temperature, and nanoscale characterization tools.

Our mission is direct: prepare students with the hands-on skills necessary to thrive immediately in Arizona’s rapidly expanding semiconductor manufacturing workforce. From cleanroom protocols to defect analysis, our students learn by doing.

Advanced Characterization & Metrology

State-of-the-art equipment available for student training, academic research, and industry collaboration.

Digital Microscopy

High-resolution imaging for defect inspection combined with rapid elemental identification capabilities.

Surface Profiling

Precise measurement of surface roughness, step heights, and film thickness down to the nanometer range.

AFM Characterization

Atomic Force Microscopy for topographical imaging and material property mapping at the atomic level.

Pressure & Temp Calibration

Industry-standard systems for calibrating sensors critical to semiconductor process control environments.

Process Training

Hands-on instruction in fundamental fabrication steps, contamination control, and cleanroom protocols.

Optical & Laser Metrology

Non-destructive testing methods utilizing advanced laser interferometry and optical techniques.
